Welcome to Global College

Founded in 2016, Global College is well-placed to serve the learning needs of students. We believe in the positive impact successful learning has in bringing out potential, restoring self-belief and creating new opportunities. Our flexible learning times - with courses taking place during the day, in the evening and at weekends - means you can fit learning around your life. As Global College's team of tutors are experts in their field, they bring the latest academic and industry knowledge of their teaching.
