Great Place For Education

Welcome to Global College Founded in 2016, Global College is well-placed to serve the learning needs of students. We believe in the positive impact successful learning has in bringing out potential, restoring self-belief and creating new opportunities. Our flexible learning times - with courses taking place during the day, in the evening and at weekends - means you can fit learning around your life. As Global College's team of tutors are experts in their field, they bring the latest academic and industry knowledge of their teaching.
Vision
To be the premier provider of world class education and skills, training for success in a global economy with our guiding principles of quality, integrity and suitability.
Mission
To provide quality technical and vocational training to empower graduates for individual development and global competitiveness.
Values
Glocal College values quality, integrity and sustainability in all we do as individuals and as teaching and learning community.
We are committed to creating an environment that promotes behaviours and decisions to advance our mission and vision for success.
